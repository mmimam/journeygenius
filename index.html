Frequently Asked Questions

How does JourneyGenius personalize my trip?

We combine your stated preferences with your travel history (if provided) and real‑time data to suggest activities and routes that align with your interests and pace. The more you use JourneyGenius, the smarter it becomes.

Can I make changes to the itinerary?

Absolutely. Plans change—we adapt. You can tweak activities, adjust timing or swap recommendations. The AI will reoptimize your route and bookings in seconds.

Do I have to pay to try JourneyGenius?

Early access to the platform is free. Once we launch publicly, we’ll offer tiered plans, including a generous free tier for casual travellers and premium features for power users.
